# Template 02: How you experience it (end user)

**Who fills it in:** the person who uses the tool day to day to decide or work (you do
not need to know how it is built inside).
**Approximate time:** 10-15 minutes.
**How:** answer in your own words below each question, in the `✍️` space. There are no
right or wrong answers: we want your real experience.

> Your view is especially valuable because it may not match what the technical team
> believes happens. That difference is exactly what we are looking for.

---

## 1. You and the tool

- **What is your role and what do you use the tool for?**
  ✍️
- **How often do you use it?** (daily, now and then...)
  ✍️

---

## 2. The first time

- **When you started, was it clear what it did and did not do?** Did someone explain it?
  ✍️

🎯 *What for:* to know whether the system introduces itself well or is learned "the
hard way".

---

## 3. What you think it does

- **In your own words, what does the system do?**
  ✍️
- **Does it decide for you or just suggest?** Who has the last word?
  ✍️

🎯 *What for:* to compare what you understand with what the system actually does.

---

## 4. When the result appears

- **What do you see exactly?** Describe it as you would to a colleague.
  ✍️
- **Do you understand it at a glance** or do you have to interpret it?
  ✍️

---

## 5. The "why"

- **Do you know why the system proposes what it proposes?** Does it explain it to you?
  ✍️

---

## 6. Do you trust it?

- **Do you usually accept what it proposes or review it carefully?**
  ✍️
- **Are there times you accept it even when you are not fully sure, out of speed or habit?**
  ✍️

🎯 *What for:* to detect whether the design pushes toward accepting without thinking.

---

## 7. When you do NOT agree

This block is of great interest to us.

- **When you do not agree with the proposal, what do you do?**
  ✍️
- **Can you change it? Is it easy or cumbersome?**
  ✍️
- **Are you asked for the reason for the change?**
  ✍️
- **Do you feel that change is used for something,** or is it lost?
  ✍️

> Example of an honest answer: "I change it with a dropdown, but they do not ask me why
> and I do not know if it reaches anyone. I get the feeling it does not matter."

---

## 8. Interruptions and alerts

- **Does the tool interrupt you at a bad moment?**
  ✍️
- **Do you get too many alerts?** Have you ended up ignoring them?
  ✍️

---

## 9. Changes

- **Have you noticed it change behavior from one day to the next without anyone telling you?**
  ✍️

---

## 10. What bothers you most / what you would change

- **If you could change one thing about how it presents or asks you for things, what would it be?**
  ✍️

---

## What you already suspect (optional)

If you think something is "badly thought out" in how it is presented to you, say it here.

✍️
